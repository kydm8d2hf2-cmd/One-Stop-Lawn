One Stop Lawn

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 17, 2026

Thank you for using One Stop Lawn ("the App," "we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web application. Please read this policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the practices described herein.

If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

We collect information you voluntarily enter into the App, including:

  • Lawn settings (ZIP code, grass type, lawn size)
  • Soil test results and notes
  • Lawn journal entries, including text, dates, and activity logs
  • Application log entries (herbicide, fertilizer, insecticide, fungicide records)
  • Photos you choose to attach to journal entries or soil test records
  • Height of cut preferences and watering records
  • Lawn measurement data (including coordinates of lawn perimeter polygons you draw on the map)
  • Rachio API key (if you choose to connect a Rachio smart sprinkler controller)
  • Feedback, bug reports, and feature requests submitted through the in-app Feedback form

This information is stored locally on your device except as described in Sections 1.3, 1.4, 1.5, 1.6, and 1.7.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

The App may automatically collect the following when you use certain features:

  • ZIP code-based location data — used solely to retrieve local weather, soil temperature, and hardiness zone information from third-party weather APIs. We do not store or transmit your precise GPS location to our servers.
  • GPS coordinates (only when you explicitly enable the "Walk Perimeter" measurement feature) — used in real time to trace the outline of your lawn. These coordinates are stored only on your device as part of your lawn measurement data and are not transmitted to our servers.
  • Device type, operating system version, browser type, and IP address — collected by our hosting providers (see Section 5) for standard server logging, security monitoring, and crash diagnostics.

1.3 Information Sent to Third-Party AI Services

When you use AI-powered features (such as "Ask AI," grass identification, weed identification, pest identification, fungus identification, soil test photo scanning, fertilizer label scanning, and automated task list generation), the App sends the following data to Anthropic, PBC ("Anthropic"), the provider of the Claude AI model:

  • Your text questions or prompts
  • Photos you choose to upload for AI analysis
  • Contextual lawn information (ZIP code, grass type, zone, lawn size) to improve response relevance

This data is transmitted via our Vercel-hosted API proxy (see Section 1.5) to Anthropic's API and is subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy, available at https://www.anthropic.com/privacy. We do not sell or share this information with any other third party for advertising purposes.

1.4 Weather and Location Data

The App retrieves weather and soil temperature data from the following third-party APIs using only your ZIP code:

  • Open-Meteo (open-meteo.com) — weather forecasts and soil temperature
  • Zippopotam.us — ZIP code to geographic coordinate conversion
  • GreenCast Online (greencastonline.com) — soil temperature reference links
  • Weather Underground (wunderground.com) — rainfall verification links
  • Phzmapi.org — USDA hardiness zone lookup

We do not share your ZIP code or any other personal information with these services beyond what is technically necessary to retrieve weather data.

1.5 API Proxy Service (Vercel)

The App uses a serverless proxy hosted on Vercel, Inc. ("Vercel") at one-stop-lawn.vercel.app to forward AI and sprinkler API requests. Vercel, as our hosting provider, may automatically log the following metadata for each request:

  • IP address of the requesting device
  • HTTP method, path, and response status code
  • Timestamp and approximate geographic region of the request
  • User agent string (browser and operating system identifier)

The contents of AI prompts and responses pass through this proxy in transit but are not stored on our servers. Vercel's data practices are governed by their Privacy Policy at https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy.

1.6 Rachio Smart Sprinkler Integration

If you choose to connect a Rachio smart sprinkler controller by entering your Rachio Personal API key in Settings, the App will:

  • Store your Rachio API key locally on your device
  • Transmit the API key, via our Vercel proxy (Section 1.5), to Rachio, Inc.'s public API (api.rach.io) to verify the connection, retrieve your controller and zone information, and fetch historical and recent watering events
  • Automatically create journal entries in the App for each zone run reported by Rachio

Data received from Rachio (device ID, zone names, run times, durations, event timestamps) is stored locally on your device as part of your lawn journal. We do not store your Rachio API key or any Rachio data on our servers. Rachio's data practices are governed by their Privacy Policy at https://rachio.com/privacy-policy.

You may disconnect this integration at any time by deleting the API key from Settings; doing so stops future automatic journal entries but does not delete journal entries already imported.

1.7 Feedback Submissions (Tally)

The in-app Feedback & Support form is hosted by Tally Forms, Inc. ("Tally"). When you submit feedback, Tally collects the content of your submission and may log your IP address, browser type, and submission timestamp. Tally's privacy practices are governed by their Privacy Policy at https://tally.so/help/privacy-policy. Submissions are forwarded to us by email for review.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide core app functionality including lawn care recommendations, scheduling, and tracking
  • Display localized weather, soil temperature, and rainfall data relevant to your lawn
  • Enable AI-powered identification and analysis features
  • Automatically import watering events from connected Rachio controllers into your lawn journal
  • Remember your settings and preferences between sessions
  • Calculate lawn measurements, fertilizer rates, and sand leveling requirements
  • Display relevant lawn care products and resources
  • Respond to your feedback, bug reports, and feature requests

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the App.

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

The majority of your data — including lawn settings, journal entries, soil test results, application logs, photos, lawn measurement coordinates, and your Rachio API key (if provided) — is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage or the device's native storage. This data:

  • Does not leave your device except as described in Sections 1.3 through 1.7
  • Persists on your device until you clear your browser or app data, uninstall the App, or manually delete entries
  • Is not backed up to any cloud service by default
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party without physical access to your device

You may export a JSON backup of your journal entries via the in-app "Email Backup" feature. This backup is transmitted as an email through your device's native email client; we do not receive or store the backup.

3.2 No User Accounts

The App does not require you to create an account or provide an email address to use any core feature. All data is tied to your local device.

3.3 Security Measures

We implement reasonable technical safeguards to protect data transmitted through AI and Rachio features, including HTTPS encryption for all API communications. The Vercel proxy restricts Rachio API calls to a narrow whitelist of read-only endpoints under /1/public/. However, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Anthropic, PBC — when you use AI features, as described in Section 1.3
  • With Rachio, Inc. — when you connect a Rachio controller, as described in Section 1.6
  • With Vercel, Inc. — as our API proxy and hosting provider, as described in Section 1.5
  • With GitHub, Inc. — the App is served from GitHub Pages, which logs standard HTTP request metadata (IP address, user agent, requested path) for security and abuse prevention. GitHub's privacy practices are governed by https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement
  • With Tally Forms, Inc. — when you submit feedback, as described in Section 1.7
  • With weather and geolocation API providers — only your ZIP code, to retrieve weather and zone data
  • As required by law — if compelled by a court order, subpoena, or other legal process
  • To protect rights and safety — if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of users or others

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Deletion

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You may:

  • Delete individual journal entries, soil tests, or application logs directly within the App
  • Disconnect Rachio integration by clearing your Rachio API key in Settings
  • Clear all App data by clearing your browser's localStorage or uninstalling the App
  • Choose not to use AI features, in which case no data is transmitted to Anthropic
  • Choose not to use Rachio integration, in which case no data is transmitted to Rachio

7.2 California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion of your personal information, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information. To exercise your rights, contact us using the information in Section 10.

7.3 European Residents (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, restrict or object to processing, and data portability. Our lawful basis for processing is legitimate interest in providing the App's functionality. To exercise your rights, contact us using the information in Section 10.

8. Data Retention

Data stored locally on your device is retained until you delete it. Data transmitted to Anthropic for AI features is subject to Anthropic's retention policies. Data transmitted to Rachio for sprinkler integration is subject to Rachio's retention policies. Server log metadata at Vercel and GitHub is retained per their respective policies. We do not retain copies of AI queries, responses, or Rachio data on our own servers.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the Feedback & Support form within the App's Settings tab.
